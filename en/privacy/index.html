Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information we may collect, how it is used, and how you may request correction or deletion.

Privacy Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact everwildrunningclub@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you actively submit, including but not limited to your preferred name, email address, WeChat ID, language preference, running background, comfortable pace, intended event, training goals, notes, and any other information you provide in communication with us.

We may also automatically collect basic access data while the website is in use, including IP address, browser type, device information, access time, visited pages, and cookie-related technical information.

How We Use Information

  • To process signup requests and first contact
  • To arrange participation, grouping, and follow-up communication
  • To respond to questions, partnership inquiries, and other contact requests
  • To maintain site operations, security, and user experience
  • To fulfill recordkeeping, management, and internal operational obligations where required by law

Retention Period

We retain personal information only for a reasonable period necessary to fulfill the purposes described above. When the information is no longer needed for signup handling, event communication, customer support, internal management, or legal compliance, we may delete, anonymize, or discontinue use of it as appropriate.

Requests to Delete or Correct Data

If you would like to review, correct, update, or delete personal information submitted through this website, please contact us at the address above. We will handle the request within a reasonable scope.
